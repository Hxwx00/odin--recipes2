Strawberry Muffins

Description:

Delicious strawberry muffin recipe. Minimum ingredients and takes less than an hour to make!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Strawberries
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Almond extract
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Add strawberries in a bowl, add small amount of flour and mix.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar together in a seperate bowl.
  3. Add a few eggs, one at a time.
  4. Beat in the vanilla and almond extract.
  5. Mix in flour and milk.
  6. Add the strawberries in the mixture.
  7. Distribute batter into a lined muffin tin.
  8. Top batter with berries and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Let muffins cool and enjoy!
