Description:

Quick and simple brownie recipe. Few ingredients and takes less than half an hour to make!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Choclate chips

Steps:

  1. Melt the Butter
  2. Add sugar into the melted butter and whisk together.
  3. Add vanilla extract and egg then mix together.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt and mix until combined.
  5. Stir in cholate chips, then scoop the dough.
  6. Place dough about 2 inches apart on a prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 7-10 minutes.
