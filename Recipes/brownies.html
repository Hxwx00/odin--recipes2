Brownies

Description:

Quick and simple brownie recipe. Few ingredients and takes less than half an hour to make!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients into large bowl.
  2. Spread batter into pan.
  3. Bake until top layer is dry, about 20-30 minutes.
  4. Let brownies cool.
